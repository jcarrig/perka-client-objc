Detailed Description

A ProgramType defines a single kind of offer made by the Merchant.

For example, a Merchant may have two ProgramTypes, one for coffee sales and another for sandwich sales. Because Customers are offered increasingly better perks as they return over time, a single ProgramType is used to aggregate multiple Program objects that have a fan-out based on ProgramTier .
