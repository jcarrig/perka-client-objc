Detailed Description

A coupon target that enables coupons based upon weather predictions.

The data-source for these targets is based on USGS forecasts. Given the inherent intra-day variance in weather forecasts, the availability of the coupon is triggered at a specific time-of-day by evaluating a metric and comparison operation (e.g. the predicted high temperature is above 100).
